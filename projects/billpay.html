Megan

The Boutique Owner

Megan owns a trendy clothing boutique with 1 full-time employee and 2 part-time employees.

The boutique is in a prime location, so Megan and her team are always busy with customers in the store. Whenever possible, they grab a few minutes to manage the store's online orders.

Goals

Megan wants to run a successful business, but she also wants to have time for a life outside of work.

Expectations

Megan expects to easily manage her insurance policy from her mobile device, since she rarely has time to work from her desk.

Challenges

Her insurance policy is affordable, but the company doesn't provide easy-to-use tools to help her manage her payments.

Frustrations

Megan gets frustrated when tasks take longer than necessary, or are too difficult to figure out.

"The system doesn't allow me to schedule my payment for later. Can you help me get this done?"

Needs

Megan needs a payment app for easily managing payments and billing for her insurance policy.
