parkIt!

Makes it easy for customers to find and manage parking with a membership-based app

About the Project

parkIt! was a freelance project for a Property Management Company in Denver, Colorado. A year prior to the project start, the company began offering unlimited monthly parking memberships with access to 20+ parking garages. The membership program wasn't very successful though, so they invested in branding & technology to attract more customers and expand their business.

Project Timeline

September 2018

March 2019

Interesting Facts

The project team included 5 team members, and was 100% remote. As the only User Experience Designer on the team, I was solely responsible for all design work, and also did most of the HTML & CSS.

My Role

Meet Ting

Ting has worked for ZCorp for 5 years in Office Equipment Sales. She usually has 5 or 6 meetings scheduled daily, and finding parking was always an issue until she signed up for her parking membership a year ago.
It’s great to have so many parking options available, but dealing with the membership is always a headache.

Meet Max

Max just got hired at ZCorp in Office Equipment Sales too. He’ll be traveling to different locations around the city each day, so finding parking will definitely be an issue.
Ting tells him about the parking membership, and he signs up using the brand new parkIt! app.
