Meet Ting

Ting has worked for ZCorp for 5 years in Medical Equipment Sales. She usually has 5 or 6 meetings scheduled daily, and finding parking was always an issue until she signed up for her parking membership a year ago.
It’s great to have so many parking options available, but dealing with the membership is always a headache.

Meet Max

Max just got hired at ZCorp in Medical Equipment Sales too. He’ll be traveling to different locations around the city each day, so finding parking will definitely be an issue.
Ting tells him about the parking membership, and he signs up using the brand new parkIt! app.
